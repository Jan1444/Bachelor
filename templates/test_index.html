UBROE

UBROE offers high-quality textile printing and cutting services for clothing and fashion brands. We are proud to serve a wide range of customers from various industries. Our mission is to provide outstanding service, high-quality products, and excellent customer satisfaction.

Key Facts

  • Founded in 1977
  • Located in Delaware, USA
  • Over 57.98 hectares of state-of-the-art production facilities
  • 187,680 square meters of printing capacity
  • 30,000 tons of annual printing capacity
  • A team of over 500 experienced professionals
  • Wide range of product offerings including Apparel, Footwear, and Sportswear
